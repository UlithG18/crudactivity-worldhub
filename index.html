About Varkun

The Varkun are a proud orcish race known for their strength, craftsmanship, and bond with fire. Other races call their homeland “The Realm of the Blood-Forges”, a harsh land of volcanic plains, iron canyons, and ancient ritual forges.

Identity & Culture

The Varkun believe strength is a responsibility, not cruelty. Every warrior forges a personal relic, weapon, mask, or totem in the sacred Blood-Forges, binding their spirit to the fire.
Their ancestors are honored in the Heartforge, a volcano-temple where fallen warriors are returned to the flames.
They live in organized clans, each known for a unique craft or way of war, but all united by honor and loyalty.

Origins

Says the first Varkun were born from the ashes of a celestial fire that struck the northern steppes, whether myth or truth, fire remains central to their identity and magic.

Rules over our kingdom

  • Strength is earned, never given.
    Every Varkûn must prove themselves through trial or battle.

  • The Forge is sacred.
    No Orc may disrespect the Blood-Forges or the spirits that dwell within them.

  • Loyalty to the Clan above all.
    Betrayal is the one crime no Orc will ever forgive.

  • Fire speaks truth.
    Important decisions must be made around the Great Forge Flame.

  • Death fuels legacy.
    Fallen warriors are reforged into relics that guide the next generations.
